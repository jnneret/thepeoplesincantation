About The People's Incantation

A poetry slam rooted in Earth’s plea for justice

A gathering. A moment for solidarity, under open skies.

Join us outdoors for an evening of unapologetic poetry, a space to speak, to listen, and to organize.

Inspired by the radical legacy of June Jordan, who wrote, spoke, and lived for the people, this event is an offering. A protest. A ritual of voice.
